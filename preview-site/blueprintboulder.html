Blueprint Boulder

Blueprint Boulder is a student-led organization at CU Boulder that partners with nonprofits to create technology solutions for social good. They focus on projects like:

Natural Highs

An interactive peer-mentoring program aimed at promoting healthy lifestyle choices and building a positive community culture.

Curls on the Block

An enrichment program designed to empower girls of color and increase engagement in STEAM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math).

Womenexus

A platform that fosters meaningful connections and professional development among women to support career and life goals.
