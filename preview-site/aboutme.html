About Me

Who I Am

Hi! I'm Luca, a Junior at CU currently studying Economics and Data Science, though I want to tranfer to Comp Sci.

I'm deeply passionate about animation/game development, and my dream would be to one day have my own studio where I produce games/animations.

I enjoy blending creative and technical skills to create compelling stories, and using these stories to help out my communities.

What Drives Me

From a young age, I've been fascinated by how games and animations can captivate audiences, evoke emotions, and create shared experiences.

I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for storytelling and social change, and I want to use my skills to create meaningful experiences that inspire and connect people.

Ultimately, I believe in testing the boundaries of who you are, to see who you could become.

What I'm Working On

VP of Data Buffs

I'm currently serving as the Vice President of Data Buffs at CU Boulder, a student-run organization focused on leveraging data science to solve real-world problems. I'm actively working on a collaborative project with CU Women's Basketball to analyze team performance and optimize strategies using advanced analytics.

Workshop Coordinator for Blueprint Boulder

As the Workshop Coordinator for Blueprint Boulder, I organize and lead workshops that equip students with technical and professional skills. From web development to project management, I strive to build an inclusive learning environment where students can grow their skillsets in order to join a future project, should they choose to.

I'm always looking to connect with people who share similar interests or want to collaborate on exciting projects. Feel free to reach out to me on any of the platforms below!
